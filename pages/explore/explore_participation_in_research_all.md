## Heading Description ##
The details of participation in research.

The following FHIR profiles are used to form the Participation in research list structure:

- [CareConnect-List-1](https://fhir.hl7.org.uk/STU3/StructureDefinition/CareConnect-List-1)
- [CareConnect-ResearchStudy-1](https://fhir.hl7.org.uk/STU3/StructureDefinition/CareConnect-ResearchStudy-1)

The following profiles are referenced from the Participation in research list structure:

- [CareConnect-Patient-1](https://fhir.hl7.org.uk/STU3/StructureDefinition/CareConnect-Patient-1)

## Patient Reference ##

The Participation in research list has a mandated subject reference to the Patient resource. This means that any exchange of the Participation in research heading data must also include the [Patient demographics List.](explore_patient_demographics.html)
